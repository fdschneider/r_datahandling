methodology
introduction Ecosystem services like pollination are affected by a variety of factors in agricultural used landscapes. This study was conducted to investigate the effects of land use intensity, local management and landscape structure on pollination services of bees, hoverflies and butterflies.
measurements
theory Pollinators can be attracted to a place due to specific flowers and/or the amount of inflorescences. When investigating pollinators the flower cover at the specific plot always has to be taken in account.
type Cover of inflorescences in square centimeters.

instruments none

procedures Cover (in cm2) was estimated for inflorescences within the subplot and inflorescences surrounding the subplot in a radius of 10m. The subplot was sized 4m times 8m. We conducted four surveys on 40 plots.
